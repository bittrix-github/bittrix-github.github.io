About the app

This here is a private joke, for starters.

Air Force 1 Shadow is my girlfriend's dream sneakers for the last couple of years now (kind of a rare item in our country) and she's finally got hers. She got two pairs - that's right, two pairs - as a matter of fact.

Since she is the biggest supporter of this career swapping of mine, i decided to congratulate her epic acquisition. And yes, her foot size is 7 and the color option on loading is her favourite one.

Thank you for caring, sweetheart.    <3

The app itself is a cool looking made-up landing page for purchasing AF1 Shadow. The 3D effect lays on preserve-3d transform-style and the perspective attribute, both in CSS. Decent javascript for targeting elements such as color and size selectors and the event listener for the mouse movement, triggering the card weaving and the popping up of things on the screen.
