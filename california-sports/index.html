About

I designed and developed this page with the primary intention to show some of my skills with SVG animations in CSS.

Needed a theme so i decided to make this kind of a homage to one of my favourite videogames while still a kid back in mid to late 90's.

This little app is called California Sports, based on the SEGA Mega Drive (SEGA Genesis) port for California Games (1991).

The bgm is controlled with this cute draggable player and features the original themes for each sport in the game - as the songs were a huge chunk of the fun in OG videogames.


Have fun. =)
