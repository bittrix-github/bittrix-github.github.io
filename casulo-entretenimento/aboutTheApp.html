This is a pseudo version of the institutional webpage for Casulo Entretenimento™.

My girlfriend (referenced in the footer, with a link to her Behance page) is a UI designer and sent me the mockup of this beautiful design she made - as a freelancer back in october 2020 - so i could practice developing front-end web applications.

A regular website with a whole bunch of different styling such as colors, blend modes, grid display and a gallery page, with this simple, functional slider so the user can navigate between the photographs of some of the events covered by Casulo Entretenimento™.

The site is fully responsive, including a mobile version for 500 pixels max devices.

The official website is available at casuloentretenimento.com.br but was not quite well developed, letting Andressa down on her neat UI original project, available at xd.adobe.com/view/casulo-desktop/ .


Tried my best to stick to the prototype, hope i nailed it. =D
