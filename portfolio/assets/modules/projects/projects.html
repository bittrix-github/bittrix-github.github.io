Projects

These here are the projects/appliccations I have been working on for the past few months, since I decided to become a web developer.

In this version 1.0 of the portfolio, the web apps were optmized for desktop Google Chrome and each one of them has information about it's responsiveness with viewport width. All the seven web dev apps were built for a 640 pixels minimum height and 1024 pixels minimum width, so if you're having any minor issue with positioning of some elements, please consider zooming out/rescaling your browser so it can fit good on your screen. Future versions of this portfolio are already on their way and all of the apps will definitely be full responsive. The first five apps have a 500 pixels mobile version.

My main focus was on front-end developing – with HTML, CSS and Javascript – but I also hosted two other projects of mine, one of them being this really cool pygame (Python) I developed a couple of months ago and the other one, the final project (in R language) of my engineering graduation back in 2018.
