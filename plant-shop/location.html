About the App

This web app is a fictional plant shop designed specifically for practicing front-end development, and it is the first application I made myself.

Found the project in Figma Community and decided to give it a try, but unfortunately, it isn't available anymore for comparison purposes (it was up here Plant-Shop-Website-(Practice)).

Given the fact that the original mockup was very simple, my girlfriend designed this cute Error 404 so the website could have an extra page and a personal touch.

Definitely I didn't develop it the best way and even now I see so many things I could have done decently better, but I'm really proud to overcome inertia and get my first app done.
